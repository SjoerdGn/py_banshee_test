NOTE:  
It could happen that an Graphviz error arises. 
To solve the problem add the Graphviz executables on your systems' PATH as follows: 

– Press the Windows key 
– Type in the search box: edit environment variables for your account 
– Select Path 
– Click Edit… button  
– Click New 
– Paste the route of the Graphviz bin folder, for example: C:\Program Files (x86)\Graphviz2.38\bin 
– Click OK and OK again 

Once have done that, restart you python IDE (if its open)
